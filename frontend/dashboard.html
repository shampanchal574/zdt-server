Everything Your Shop
Needs to Bill Smarter

Purpose-built for Indian shop owners. From kirana to electronics — ZDT works for every kind of retail.

🧾

One-Click Invoicing

Generate GST-compliant invoices in seconds. Customise templates with your logo and send via WhatsApp or email instantly.

📊

Real-Time Analytics

Track daily sales, top-selling products, and revenue trends on a live dashboard. Know your shop's pulse every hour.

GST Auto-Calculate

Automatically compute CGST, SGST, and IGST. Export GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B reports with one click — no accountant needed.

Multi-Payment Tracking

Track UPI, cash, card, and credit payments in one place. Auto-reconcile and mark invoices paid with zero friction.

Inventory Integration

Connect billing with stock levels. Get low-stock alerts, auto-deduct sold items, and never oversell again.

Smart Reminders

Automated WhatsApp reminders for outstanding invoices — polite, professional, and customisable for every customer.
